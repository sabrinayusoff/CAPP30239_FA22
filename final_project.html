Most people drive alone to work

Majority of working adults over 15 years old travel to work by driving alone, followed by taking public transportation. Interestingly, a large proportion of residents with lower earnings (between $1 to $34,999) drive alone despite it being a more expensive mode of transport. There may be various explanations for this, one of which could be the inadequacy of public transportation in bringing them to work. US Census (American Community Survey)
